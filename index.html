 Projects

Move Machine

The Movie Machine is an app that provides you a list of recommended titles based on one of your favorite films. Each result provides cast, rating, and plot information, along with MovieClips from YouTube, and links to places to stream or purchase online.

Tech Stack: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery

Demo GitHub

The Fintech Guru Expense Tracker

The Fintech Guru Expense Tracker is a simple expense tracker that gives users the ability to track, manage, and target their monthly expenses over the course of a full year.

Tech Stack: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Node, Express, BCryptJS, JWT, and Passport.

Demo GitHub

The Good Life

The Good Life is a retirement tracker app that allows users to target the amount they need for retirement and track or adjust their savings to meet that goal..

Tech Stack: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Redux, Node, Express, BCryptJS, JWT, and Passport.
