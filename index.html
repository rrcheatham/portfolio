I'm Reed, a front-end developer & design enthusiast based out of Atlanta, GA.

After five years in finance, I am starting a career in web development
having recently graduated from the Full Stack Developer bootcamp at Thinkful.
I enjoy taking the problem solving skills I developed with financial modeling
to a new level in coding dynamic, exciting web applications.

I find the coding process very rewarding from designing the look and feel of a site
to building its efficient functionality in order to create an exciting user experience.
I am always learning and love to be introduced to new concepts, frameworks, design trends,
and ideas. When I am not coding I enjoy volunteering, meet-ups with the great tech scene in Atlanta, and hiking.
